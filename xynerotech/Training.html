Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our training programs

Who are these training programs for?

Our programs are designed for students, working professionals, and career switchers. We offer courses at beginner, intermediate, and advanced levels. Whether you're just starting in tech or looking to upskill, we have a program for you.

What is the duration of the courses?

Course durations vary from 4-week intensive programs to 6-month comprehensive bootcamps. Most of our popular courses run for 8-12 weeks with 10-15 hours of weekly commitment including live sessions and self-paced learning.

Do you offer placement assistance?

Yes! Our career services include resume building, interview preparation, and connecting students with our hiring partners. Many of our programs include guaranteed interview opportunities with tech companies.

What are the prerequisites for enrollment?

Prerequisites vary by course. Beginner courses typically require no prior experience, while advanced programs may require foundational knowledge. Each course listing includes specific prerequisites and we offer free assessment tests to help you determine the right starting point.

Are there any financing options available?

We offer several financing options including installment plans, no-cost EMIs, and scholarships for eligible students. We also partner with financing companies to provide education loans at competitive rates.

Can I access course materials after completion?

Yes, students retain lifetime access to course materials, including video recordings, code repositories, and learning resources. We also provide free updates to course content as technologies evolve.
